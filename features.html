Every feature your ops team needs

TradeRecon is purpose-built for the post-trade lifecycle — from multi-source ingestion through exception resolution and compliance reporting.

01 / Data Ingestion

Drag & Drop File Upload

Upload CSV, JSON or XML trade blotters from any OMS or EMS. Files validated on arrival against schema rules.

Manual Trade Entry

Structured form for bespoke or OTC trades. All fields validated in real time including ISIN, CUSIP, and notional.

Schema Validation

Field-level and cross-field validation rules catch errors before they enter the reconciliation engine.

02 / Reconciliation Engine

Multi-Asset Matching

Equities, fixed income, FX, commodities, and derivatives matched against counterparty data with configurable tolerances.

7 Exception Types

Price mismatch, settlement date, currency, quantity, duplicate, missing confirmation, and commission discrepancies.

Severity Scoring

Automatic Critical / High / Medium / Low classification based on variance magnitude and regulatory impact.

03 / Reporting & Compliance

Match Rate Analytics

Portfolio-level dashboards showing match rates, open exceptions, and trends across counterparties.

One-Click CSV Export

Export trade lists, exception summaries, or full audit logs as CSV for compliance and external review.

Immutable Audit Log

Every action time-stamped and attributed. Full trail from ingestion to resolution for MiFID II and EMIR.
