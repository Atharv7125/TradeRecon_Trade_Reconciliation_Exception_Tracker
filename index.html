Platform Capabilities

Everything ops teams need

From multi-format ingestion to regulatory-grade audit trails — TradeRecon covers the full exception lifecycle.

Multi-Format Ingestion

Upload CSV, JSON, or XML trade files. Manual entry form for bespoke trades. All data validated on arrival.

Intelligent Exception Detection

Auto-detect price mismatches, settlement discrepancies, duplicate trades, missing confirmations, and more.

Rule-Based Matching Engine

Configurable tolerances per asset class. ISIN, CUSIP, and counterparty-level matching with variance tracking.

Assign exceptions to traders or ops staff. Comment threads, status workflows — Open → In Progress → Resolved.

Match rate trends, exception type breakdowns, portfolio-level views. One-click CSV export for compliance.

Full Audit Trail
