Our Story

Built for ops teams, by ops veterans

TradeRecon was founded by a team of post-trade operations specialists who spent a collective 40 years manually chasing breaks in spreadsheets. We built the tool we always wished existed.

Our Mission

Capital markets settlement failures cost the global industry billions annually. The root cause is almost never the trades themselves — it's the manual, fragmented processes used to identify and resolve the breaks afterward.

TradeRecon's mission is to make every trade reconciliation team as fast, accurate, and defensible as the best-resourced prime brokerage desks — regardless of firm size.
